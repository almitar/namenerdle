How To Play

Guess the name in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a 5-letter name listed in the SSA database.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the name.
  • The spelling of the name will be the most widely used in the US.

Examples

LORNA - L is in the word and in the correct spot.

PAULA - A is in the word but in the wrong spot.

CAROL - O is not in the word in any spot.
